後京極摂政前(ごきょうごくせっしょうさきの) 太政大臣(だいじょうだいじん)

Go Kyōgoku Sesshō Saki no|Daijōdaijin

Kirigirisu
Naku ya shimo-yo no
Samushiro ni
Koromo kata shiki
Hitori kamo nen

In my cold bed,
Drawing close my folded quilt,
I sleep alone,
While all through the frosty night
I hear a cricket's lonely sound.
