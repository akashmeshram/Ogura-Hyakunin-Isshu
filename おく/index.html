Ogura Hyakunin Isshu

5

猿丸大夫(さるまるだゆう)

Sarumaru Dayū

Oku-yama ni
Momiji fumi wake
Naku shika no
Koe kiku toki zo
Aki wa kanashiki

In the mountain depths,
Treading through the crimson leaves,
The wandering stag calls.
When I hear the lonely cry,
Sad (how sad!) the autumn is.
