Ogura Hyakunin Isshu

74

源俊頼朝臣(みなもとのとしよりあそん)

Minamoto no Toshiyori Ason

Ukari keru
Hito o hatsuse no
Yama-oroshi yo
Hageshi kare to wa
Inoranu monoo

It was not for this
I prayed at the holy shrine:
That she would become
As pitiless and as cold
As the storms on Hase's hills.
