Ogura Hyakunin Isshu

22

文屋康秀(ふんやのやすひで)

Fun\'ya no Yasuhide

Fuku karani
Aki no kusaki no
Shiorureba
Mube yama-kaze o
Arashi to iu ran

It is by its breath
That autumn's leaves of trees and grass
Are wasted and driven.
So they call this mountain wind
The wild one, the destroyer.
