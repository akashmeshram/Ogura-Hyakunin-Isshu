左京大夫顕輔(さきょうのだいぶあきすけ)

Sakyō no Daibu Akisuke

Aki-kaze ni
Tanabiku kumo no
Taema yori
More izuru tsuki no
Kage no sayakesa

See how clear and bright
Is the moonlight finding ways
Through the riven clouds
That, with drifting autumn wind,
Gracefully float in the sky.
